Know Your Rights – The Skater's Guide

This guide was created by Sunny, a skater turned law student, passionate about using the law to empower the scene that raised him.

About Sunny

Sunny grew up skating South London spots and later went on to study law. Now on the path to becoming a barrister, he’s blending street-level culture with legal education to give skaters the tools to protect themselves and each other.

Whether you’re out filming clips or just chilling at your local, this guide helps you understand the rights you’ve got — and how to use them confidently.

The Code of Lawyer Skaters

  • Respect the session — but know your legal rights if it's shut down
  • Know the law — so you can protect your friends and your freedom
  • Use your platform — teach others and pass it on
  • Build, don’t just destroy — the system’s broken, but we’re not helpless
